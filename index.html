ABOUT Firebase event :
It was an event about Architecting Scalable Applications using Firebase. The first half of the event was a seminar in which the speaker explains about the architecture of an application and applications of firebase in handling database, security,etc. The second half was the hands-on where the participants build the simple architecture of their application using Firebase.

ABOUT Devops event :
It was a great knowledge gaining experience spread over a course of 2 days. In both the sessions, speakers had taken the participants to the tour of devops platforms such as github, docker and puppet. Speakers used to give insights of a particular devops platform to the participants and then hands-on for the same was conducted.

ABOUT Blockchain event :
It was a great knowledge gaining experience spread over a course of 2 days. Our speaker Kedar Rane gave insights about blockchain and it's related concepts on the first day. The next day was hands-on where participants implemented the concepts that they learned on the first day.

ABOUT GOOGLE STUDY JAM :
With a crowd of 200+ we kick-started this year with the grand Google and Pydata Study Jam event. Students were given one month access to Google Cloud Platform through which they gained Google certification. The Pizzas were sponsored by Pydata for lunch and at the end we had a kahoot quiz for engagement of the crowd !!
